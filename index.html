how to play the game


  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.

  • In each turn, a player rolls two dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his CURRENT score.

  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1 on any of the dice, all his CURRENT score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.

  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.

  • A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls two 6 in a row on any of the dice. After that, it's the next player's turn.

  • The first player to reach Winner score entered by user on GLOBAL score wins the game.
